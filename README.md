##  Projects

### 1) IPL Cricket Analysis
**Tools:** MySQL, Tableau  
**Dataset:** 950+ matches · 200,000+ ball-by-ball delivery records (2008–2022)

**Description:** End-to-end analysis of IPL performance data using two relational tables, match-level and ball-by-ball deliveries. Wrote 8 SQL queries involving multi-table joins, subqueries, aggregations, and CASE logic to extract insights across batting, bowling, team performance, and match conditions. Built a 6-chart Tableau dashboard with intentional diverging color encoding to communicate rankings at a glance.

**Key Findings:**
- Mumbai Indians lead all-time wins (140+) with the highest win rate among active franchises
- Virat Kohli tops run scorers by a ~1,500 run margin; YS Chahal leads wicket takers with 200+
- Average first innings scores rose from ~150 (2008) to ~189 (2022), reflecting the T20 batting evolution

### 2) Hotel Booking Cancellation Analysis
**Tools:** MySQL, Tableau  
**Dataset:** 119,390 booking records across city and resort hotels

**Description:** Analyzed booking cancellation patterns across hotel type, market segment, country, lead time, and deposit type. Wrote 9 SQL queries to isolate the highest-risk cancellation segments, then built a 7-chart interactive Tableau dashboard to surface actionable pricing and policy insights.

**Key Findings:**
- Non-refundable deposit bookings had the highest cancellation rate — a counterintuitive pricing policy risk
- Bookings made 3+ months in advance cancel at nearly 2x the rate of last-minute bookings
- Online travel agencies drive the highest booking volume but also the highest cancellation share
